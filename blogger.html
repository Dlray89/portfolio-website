Life is your stage!
written on: 01/10/2020

You ever just sit back and think about how life is very artistic and dramatic. We all know about the tough challenges that we face after we leave our parent's house. I'm 30 years of age and in my twenty. I've realized one thing, and that was Life was literally like a live stage. Look I came to this realization that I needed to change a few things about myself after reading this quote. Let me share the quote with you.

The quote said:

All the world's a stage, and all the men and women merely players: they have their exits and their entrances, And one man in his time plays many parts, His acts being seven ages by William Shakespeare

We are all merely actors in this play called life, and we must know what roles to play to live the life we want to live. If you don't know what roles you are supposed to play in life and good play to start and look is back at your past and look into your future. Be who you want to be but make sure you don't lose yourself in the process.
