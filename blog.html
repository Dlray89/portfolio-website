Developer's Web

Written by: DAP

As I sit here and ponder where my life is headed in the next few months, I realized that I am growing tremendously in life. I see the world a lot different
and I'm able to handle tough challenges that life herself throws at me. One of the biggest things that stood out is how I'm caught in this what I like to call the developer's web.
You see once I decide to get into web development and Network security it has out my determination on a whole new level. I became obsessed with front end/back end designs and all aspect of network
security. I love how the front end of web development challenges my creativity and back end challenges my technical skills, which I love. You see this web has given me a tangible skill that has added
value in my life. I taught myself how to code and things got a little too complicated. So I hopped online to look up ways to better learn and understand the back end side of web development. Lambda School
was the first to pop up. I simply love what they had to offer and I saw it as an opportunity to upgrade my current skills and learn new ones.

With that being said my journal has been exciting and I can't honestly wait to see what I can accomplish here in the next few weeks.
